About JanSahayak

Multi-Agent Government Intelligence System

What is JanSahayak?

JanSahayak (जन सहायक - "People's Assistant") is an advanced AI-powered system designed to help Indian citizens discover and access government benefits, schemes, and competitive exam opportunities. Using state-of-the-art multi-agent architecture, we analyze your profile and provide personalized recommendations.

How It Works

1

Profile Extraction

AI extracts structured information from your input using natural language processing

2

Intelligent Search

RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation) + live web search finds relevant schemes and exams

3

Personalization

7 specialized AI agents work together to provide tailored recommendations

4

Benefit Analysis

Calculate missed benefits and get actionable insights for future applications
